RK

QR Code Generator

Generate QR codes for train seats

Configuration

This will generate QR codes for all seats in the range

How to use

  • Enter train number and coach details
  • Set seat range (e.g., 1 to 72 for Sleeper class)
  • Click "Generate QR Codes"
  • Print and laminate the QR codes
  • Stick one QR code on each seat/table
  • When scanned, customer will see feedback form pre-filled with seat details
